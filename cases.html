Cyber Threat Cases

Exploring significant cybersecurity incidents and how they were resolved

WannaCry Ransomware Attack

May 2017

A global ransomware attack that affected over 200,000 computers across 150 countries. The attack exploited a vulnerability in Windows systems.

Resolution:

Security researchers discovered a "kill switch" that halted the spread, and Microsoft released emergency patches for unsupported systems.

SolarWinds Supply Chain Attack

December 2020

A sophisticated supply chain attack that compromised the software build process of SolarWinds, affecting numerous government agencies and corporations.

Resolution:

Extensive investigation led to identifying the breach vectors, and organizations implemented enhanced supply chain security measures.

Equifax Data Breach

September 2017

One of the largest data breaches in history, exposing sensitive information of approximately 147 million people.

Resolution:

Equifax implemented comprehensive security reforms, established a $700 million compensation fund, and enhanced their monitoring systems.
